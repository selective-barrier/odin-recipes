Description

This lasagna recipe features layers of sweet Italian sausage and lean ground beef simmered with onions and garlic in a rich tomato sauce, seasoned with herbs like basil and Italian seasoning. The sauce is layered with lasagna noodles and a creamy mixture of ricotta, mozzarella, and Parmesan cheeses, then baked until bubbly and golden, resulting in a hearty and comforting dish.

Ingredients

  • Meat
  • Onion and garlic
  • Tomato products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and seasonings
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Cheeses
  • Egg

Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce.
  2. Cook the noodles.
  3. Make the ricotta mixture.
  4. Layer the lasagna according to the recipe instructions.
  5. Let the lasagna rest before serving.
Layering Lasagna
  1. Meat sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Ricotta mixture
  4. Mozzarella slices
  5. Meat sauce
  6. Parmesan cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, then top with the remaining Parmesan.
