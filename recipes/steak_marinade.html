Steak Marinade recipe

Description

The “Best Steak Marinade in Existence” is a flavorful blend designed to tenderize and enhance the taste of various beef cuts. It combines olive oil, soy sauce, lemon juice, Worcestershire sauce, dried basil, garlic powder, dried parsley flakes, white pepper, minced garlic, and optional hot pepper sauce. This marinade not only imparts a rich, savory flavor but also helps in tenderizing the meat. For optimal results, marinate thinner cuts like skirt or flank steaks for 2-4 hours, and tougher cuts such as top sirloin or sirloin tip for up to 8 hours before grilling or cooking to your preferred doneness.

Ingredients

  • Olive oil
  • Balsamic vinegar
  • Sauces
  • Seasonings

Steps

  1. Pour the marinade into a resealable plastic bag
  2. Add the steak, coat with the marinade, squeeze out the excess air, and seal the bag. Marinate in the refrigerator for at least two hours
  3. Remove steak from the marinade and shake off excess
  4. Discard the remaining marinade. Cook or grill steaks to desired doneness
